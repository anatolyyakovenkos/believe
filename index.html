The Thesis

Why attention is the only metric.

👁

Eyeballs = Value

Every protocol, every token, every project is fundamentally a vehicle for attention. The ones that capture the most eyes win. Everything else is downstream.

⚡

Speed Kills

Attention moves at the speed of a scroll. You have milliseconds to capture it, seconds to hold it, and minutes before it moves to the next thing. The fast eat the slow.

💡

Narratives Are Weapons

A good narrative is an attention magnet. It gives people something to believe in, something to repeat, something to spread. Narratives are how attention compounds.

🌊

Virality Is The Moat

You can't buy sustained attention. You earn it through content that people can't help but share. Virality is the only defensible advantage in the attention economy.

🎯

Focus Is Alpha

Everyone's attention is scattered. The edge goes to those who can focus theirs — and direct others'. Concentrated attention is the most powerful force in markets.

🔥

Memes > Metrics

A meme that captures attention is worth more than a whitepaper nobody reads. In the attention economy, what spreads wins. What doesn't spread dies.

The Attention Cycle

First comes the spark — a tweet, a meme, a screenshot that makes you stop scrolling.

Then comes the cascade — retweets, quote tweets, threads, everyone trying to be the one who explains it best.

Then comes the FOMO — the chart goes up because people are looking, and people look because the chart goes up.

Then comes the shift — attention moves to the next thing. Always. Without exception.

The game isn't picking what to pay attention to. The game is understanding that attention itself is the trade.
